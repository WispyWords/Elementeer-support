Welcome to the official support hub for Elementeer App! We're here to help you with any questions, issues, or feedback you might have.


How to Get Support

Before opening a new issue, please take a moment to:


Check Existing Issues/Discussions: Someone else might have already reported the same issue or asked a similar question.

Searching through existing entries can often provide a quick solution.

For Bug Reports: Please open a new "Issue" and provide the following details:

Go To: https://github.com/WispyWords/Elementeer/issues/new
What you did: Describe the steps you took that led to the bug.
What you expected to happen: Describe the correct behavior.
What actually happened: Describe the incorrect behavior you observed.
Screenshots/Videos (if applicable): Visuals are incredibly helpful!
Your operating system and browser (if applicable): e.g., Windows 10, Chrome latest; iOS 17, Safari.
